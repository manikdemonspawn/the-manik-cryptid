Meet The Cryptid

My name is Cryptid.

I write things that bite back.

Some are love stories.

Some are horror stories.

Most are both.

I collect strange thoughts, Appalachian shadows, impossible romances, monsters, ghosts, folklore, and things people usually keep hidden.

This place exists because stories matter. Sometimes a single sentence follows someone for years. I like writing those sentences.

Welcome to the woods.
